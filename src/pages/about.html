About testing

A minimal SPA framework built for creative devs who want full control over motion, scroll, and Three.js — without the overhead.

One RAF loop. One renderer. Sections that know where they live. No wrappers, no virtual DOM, no magic. Just code you can read. The scroll engine is 60 lines. The RAF is 15. The router is 150. Every piece is replaceable.

We believe frameworks should disappear into the project. Not the other way around.

  • Vite + vanilla JS/TS — no build magic you can't explain
  • Three.js WebGL — one renderer, per-page scenes, shared camera
  • Custom fake-scroll engine — damp frame-rate independent, virtualized sections
  • SSR / SSG via NDJSON streaming — body first, cache second
  • SPA router — clip-path transitions, prefetch, 15s safety valve
  • Loader — tracks images + Three.js textures, numeric progress

No abstraction without a reason. No dependency you didn't choose. No lifecycle you can't follow by reading entry-client.js top to bottom.

The scroll engine doesn't know about Three.js. Three.js doesn't know about the router. The router doesn't know about scroll. Each piece does one thing.

When something breaks, you know where to look.

No dependencies you didn't ask for. No conventions you can't override. No magic you can't delete.
